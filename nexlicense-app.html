    The provider commits to delivering the agreed project, license activation, or feature rollout within the timeline confirmed in writing at the time of order. Any deviation must be disclosed to the client as soon as it is identified.

    If the confirmed delivery date is missed for reasons attributable to the provider, the client is entitled to a full refund of all amounts paid for the affected order, with no deductions or penalties, upon request.

    3. Full Refund on Unapproved Price Increase

    Prices are locked at the time of order confirmation. If the provider raises the price of an active plan or add-on without the client's prior written approval, the client may cancel and receive a full refund of the affected charges.

    Each licensed seat is bound by the feature access, monthly usage count, word/character volume, and duration limits configured by the admin. Limits reset or expire according to the plan cycle shown on the user's profile.
